PWM Formulas

Duty Cycle (%): \( \text{Duty Cycle} = \frac{\text{ON Time}}{\text{Period}} \times 100 \)

ON Time (ms): \( \text{ON Time} = \frac{\text{Duty Cycle} \times \text{Period}}{100} \)

Frequency (Hz): \( \text{Frequency} = \frac{1}{\text{Period}} \)

Numerical Examples:

  • Duty Cycle: \( 89\% \)
  • ON Time: \( 8.9 \, \text{ms} \, (\text{for a 10 ms period}) \)
  • Frequency: 328.125 kHz (for a 255-count period at 84 MHz)
